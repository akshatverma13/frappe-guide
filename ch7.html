Dependency Order — What to Create First

⚠️
Always follow this sequence

For initial system setup, data imports, testing, and mobile sync. Transaction forms reference master data — they will fail if the referenced record doesn't exist yet.

LevelContainsCreate When
Level 0All 6 Geography DocTypes + 30+ Option MastersFirst — no dependencies on anything
Level 1Employee Master, Household Survey, Volunteers, EnrolmentAfter Level 0 is complete
Level 2All DVRs, MIS forms, Attendance, Headcount, Student ProfilingAfter Level 1 is complete
Level 3Assessment Form, Baseline-Midline-EndlineAfter Level 2 is complete
