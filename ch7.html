Dependency Order — What to Create First

⚠️
Follow this order always

For initial setup, data import, testing, and mobile sync. Transaction forms reference master data — they fail if the reference doesn't exist yet.

LevelWhat It ContainsCreate When
Level 0All 6 Geography DocTypes + 30+ Option MastersFirst — no dependencies
Level 1Employee Master, Household Survey, Volunteers, EnrolmentAfter Level 0
Level 2All DVRs, MIS forms, Attendance, Headcount, Student ProfilingAfter Level 1
Level 3Assessment Form, Baseline-Midline-EndlineAfter Level 2

A DVRs first — they are the most important forms
